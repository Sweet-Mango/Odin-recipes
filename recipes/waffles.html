Waffles

Description:

This recipe will help you create fluffy and warm waffles with syrup and butter on top.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1½ cups of warm milk
  • ⅓ cup of butter, melted
  • ⅓ cup of butter, cold
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • ½ cup of syrup
